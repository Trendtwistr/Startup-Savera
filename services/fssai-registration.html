Introduction to FSSAI Registration

FSSAI registration is mandatory for all food businesses in India involved in manufacturing, storing, transporting, or distributing food. It ensures compliance with food safety standards and provides a 14-digit number that must be displayed on food packaging. Issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI), this license offers legal credibility, promotes food safety, and supports business growth. The registration process is governed by the Food Safety and Standards (Licensing and Registration of Food Business) Regulations, 2011.

Benefits of FSSAI Registration

Ensures compliance with food safety regulations.

Builds customer trust and enhances credibility.

Opens up opportunities for business growth and expansion.

Promotes quality assurance through food safety management practices.

Strengthens market reputation and improves brand image.

Who Needs to Register for FSSAI?

  • Petty retailers, retail shops, snack shops, confectionery or bakery shops
  • Temporary stalls, fixed stalls, or food premises involved in food preparation, storage, distribution, and sale
  • Hawkers selling packaged or freshly prepared food
  • Dairy units, including milk chilling units, petty milkmen, and milk vendors
  • Slaughterhouses
  • Fish and meat processing units
  • Food manufacturing units, including repacking food
  • Vegetable oil processing units
  • Proprietary and novel food businesses
  • Cold/refrigerated storage facilities
  • Transporters of food products using specialized vehicles (e.g., refrigerated vans, milk tankers, food trucks)
  • Wholesalers, suppliers, distributors, and marketers of food products
  • Hotels, restaurants, and bars
  • Canteens, cafeterias, and mid-day meal providers
  • Food vending agencies and caterers
  • Dhaba, PGs, banquet halls, home-based canteens, and food stalls at fairs/religious institutions
  • Importers and exporters of food items and ingredients
  • E-commerce food suppliers, including cloud kitchens

Difference Between FSSAI Registration and FSSAI License

Particulars FSSAI Registration FSSAI License
Turnover Limit For businesses with annual turnover up to Rs. 12 lakh For businesses with annual turnover above Rs. 12 lakh
Types Basic Registration FSSAI Central License or FSSAI State License
Size of Business Small food businesses or petty food operators Medium to large-sized food businesses
Duration Depends on the registration type Valid for 1 to 5 years
Application Form A Form B
Publication FSSAI registration number must be displayed on premises and product packaging FSSAI license number must be displayed on product packaging, especially for exporters, importers, and manufacturers
